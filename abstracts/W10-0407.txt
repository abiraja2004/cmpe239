 
Rather than explain research that has al-ready been carried out, this paper describes a specific context of writing instruction and poses questions about how research on writing and computational linguistics might be brought together to address three press-ing issues: the validity of Directed Self-Placement; the relationship between confi-dence and competence in student writing; and strategies to help English Language Learners, especially those in the category of Generation 1.5, improve their writing. 1

 The system to spot INIs, DNIs and their anteced-ents is an adaptation of VENSES, a system for semantic evaluation that has been used for RTE challenges in the last 6 years. In the following we will briefly describe the system and then the ad-ditions we made to cope with the new task. In particular, we will discuss how we mapped the VENSES analysis to the representation of frame information in order to identify null instantia-tions in the text. 1
